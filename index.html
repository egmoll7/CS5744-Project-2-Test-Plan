We have chosen to test the Pet Adoption and Medical Record System Project by Daniel Slutsky, Jonathan Dixon and Wenjia Song. This Test Plan will be based off a simple High-Level Design of the system, and will not have the full detailed information needed to completely implement the testing that we believe is necessary to show the sucess of the Pet Adoption and Medical Record application.

Summary

The Pet Adoption and Medical Record System is an application that helps to file our pets documents in one place. It hopes to organize and store all of our pets records, not just their medical records in one database that can be used by many different users. They are planning to build an application where any Animal Adoption Center, Veterinarians or Pet Owners can access and, to different extends, contribute to the file of an animal. The goal is to offer the best way to take care of and keep the animal healthy and to share or better understand any issues that the pet might have. This system has the potential to pair animals with families and organizations that can help take the best care of the animal. It can also clear up any medical mysteries from having to be solved more than once, and even save a pets life. Testing needs to be set up to ensure that only certain functions are accessible by different users. Our testing will focus on how the application will allow different users to interact at different levels of the application and the pet database.
